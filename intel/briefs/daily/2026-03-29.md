## 🔴 Crashes & Stability

### 1. Physics Explosion with Custom Ackermann AMR (Isaac Sim 5.1.0)
- **URL:** https://forums.developer.nvidia.com/t/sample-ackermann-custom-car-physics-explosion-on-movement/364774
- **Version:** Isaac Sim 5.1.0
- **Severity:** blocker
- **Source:** NVIDIA Forum · 2026-03-25
- **Summary:** Custom Ackermann car (OnShape → USD) explodes/flies apart on physics start. Stock F1Tenth.usd works fine; issue isolated to imported custom USD with ROS bridge active. Likely joint/pivot configuration problem during import, but user followed the sample-ackermann-amr tutorial exactly.

- **URL:** https://forums.developer.nvidia.com/t/omni-datastore-error/364867

- **Severity:** pain
- **Source:** NVIDIA Forum (Isaac ROS) · 2026-03-26

### 3. Mecanum Wheel Jitter in Imported USD (Isaac Sim 5.1.0)
